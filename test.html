Missouri Window Tint Laws

Know the state regulations before you tint

  • Windshield: Non-reflective tint allowed on the top 6 inches.
  • Front Side Windows: Must allow more than 35% of light in.
  • Back Side Windows: Any darkness allowed.
  • Rear Window: Any darkness allowed.
  • Mirrors: Dual side mirrors required if rear window is tinted.
  • Restricted Colors: No metallic or mirrored tint.

* These laws are based on Missouri state regulations. Always confirm with local authorities before installation. Which can be found at the Missouri Tint Laws page
